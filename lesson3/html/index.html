Site Title

The site title will be "Weather Fortune". As the name indicates, this is a weather forecasting site.

Site Purpose

This site's purpose is to provide users with up-to-date and relevant weather information for the three towns of Preston, Fish Haven, and Soda Springs. The site will provide a current weather story with corresponding images and information.

The site will also show contact information and a location map along with copyright information, the author's name, a terms of use link, and the current date.

Domain Name

The domain name is weatherfortune.com and it currently costs $12 per year.

Logo

This is the Weather Fortune site logo.

The logo is bright and inviting that also fits the purpose of the site. It is a vector image so it does not blur.
