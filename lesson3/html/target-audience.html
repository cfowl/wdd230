Audience description

  • Who: Anyone who wants to know what the weather is so they can plan a trip, know what to wear, and be prepared.
  • Age: 16-100
  • Income: All incomes
  • Motivation: Learn today's weather forecast and learn the 14-day forecast so they can be prepared.

Two Personas

Richard Merrill

  • Occupation: Retired mailman
  • Demographics: 65 year old male, with a retirement income of $48,500
  • Motivation: To arrange outdoor meetings with other seniors.
  • Social: Richard is part of a retirment community that meets every Saturday. Richard wants to know the 5-day forecast so he can plan the meeting outdoors, if possible. If the weather is bad, Richard has to reserve the local social hall.
  • Technology: Richard uses a PC desktop computer to check the weather, browse the intenet, and email grandchildren.

Jenni Larsen

  • Occupation: Part-time personal trainer
  • Demographics: 34 year old female, with a combined income of $62,000. Jenni is a mother and a wife.
  • Motivation: Jenni like to take outdoor vacations with her husband in the spring and fall (when the weather is least predictable). Jenni also likes to have her clients exercise outdoors as much as possible during the spring, summer, and fall.
  • Social: Jenni is so busy as a mother, wife, and part-time personal trainer that she only has time to communicate with her friends on their Facebook and Instagram pages
  • Technology: Jenni mostly uses her smartphone to check weather and browse the internet, but she also owns a laptop.

Scenarios

  • What is the weather going to be next Saturday?
  • How do I contact the Weather Fortune team to ask a question or give a suggestion?
  • How often is the weather forecast updated?
  • Will it rain or snow this week?
  • What are the average low and high temparatures for the week?
  • How windy is it going to be tonight?
  • What time does is the sunset tonight?
  • Is tonight a full moon?
  • When is it going to stop raining today?
  • What will be the temparture tomorrow morning at 0600 hours?
