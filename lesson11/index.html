A Message of Peace

by Chris Fowler

The following is a snippet of a Christmas message written by Chris Fowler in 2018:

"...in these latter days, when the world is in commotion, when there are wars among nations, upheaval across the land, contention within families, feelings of hopelessness and loneliness among many, I pray that you, and I, and all mankind will seek Him, even He who is the Prince of Peace. “For unto us a child is born, unto us a son is given: and the government shall be upon his shoulder: and his name shall be called Wonderful, Counsellor, The mighty God, The everlasting Father, the Prince of Peace.” (Isaiah 9:6)

I pray that peace will be published in all the world, that wars will cease now as they have before, that nations will prosper in peace, that families will be made whole, that hearts will be healed. I pray that relationships will be mended, that the peace of forgiveness will be a gift that is freely given by all and to all. I pray that the peace of His Holy Spirit will enter into all hearts, into your heart and into my heart, giving peace unto our souls. I pray that those who seek peace shall find it. For God has said “Peace, peace be unto you, because of your faith in my Well Beloved, who was from the foundation of the world.” (Helaman 5:47)

After Alma prophesied that Christ shall be born of Mary, he said “And now, may the peace of God rest upon you, and upon your houses and lands, and upon your flocks and herds, and all that you possess, your women and your children, according to your faith and good works, from this time forth and forever.” (Alma 7:27)..."
